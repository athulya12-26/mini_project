Content-Based Filtering

How it works: Uses TF-IDF + Cosine Similarity on movie genres, descriptions, and year to find movies similar to your chosen title.

Collaborative Filtering

How it works: Computes User-User Cosine Similarity on a rating matrix, then recommends movies that similar users enjoyed but you haven't seen.

In a real app, this would be your account. Here, 20 simulated users demonstrate collaborative filtering.

Year & Genre Search

How it works: Filters movies by your chosen genre and year range (±5 years), returning the highest-rated matches.

Hybrid Recommendation

How it works: Combines Content-Based + Collaborative Filtering + Genre/Year Filter for the most comprehensive recommendations.
